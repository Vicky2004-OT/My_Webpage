Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about switching from MailChimp to SendMails

Will my email deliverability be affected if I switch?

No. SendMails uses Amazon SES, which powers email delivery for millions of businesses worldwide with 99.9% deliverability rates. Our platform provides advanced features like custom tracking domains, DMARC support, and smart delivery optimization to ensure your emails reach the inbox.

How does the migration process work?

We provide detailed documentation and video tutorials for migrating from MailChimp. The process involves exporting your subscribers, importing them to SendMails, and setting up your sending domain. Our documentation covers each step in detail.

What happens to my existing automations?

While automations need to be recreated in SendMails, our platform offers an intuitive workflow builder. We provide documentation and templates to help you rebuild your automation sequences effectively.

Is Amazon SES reliable enough for my business?

Absolutely. Amazon SES is trusted by enterprises worldwide and handles billions of emails monthly. It's the same infrastructure used by Amazon.com and countless Fortune 500 companies. SendMails adds additional reliability through smart delivery optimization and monitoring.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer email support Monday through Friday. Our help center includes comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and guides to help you get started and resolve common questions.

How does your pricing compare to MailChimp?

With SendMails, you pay once and own it forever. Using Amazon SES, you'll pay about $1 per 10,000 emails sent. Compare this to MailChimp's monthly fees that increase with your subscriber count. Most businesses save 90-97% on their email marketing costs.

Can I manage multiple brands or domains?

Yes! Unlike MailChimp, which requires separate paid accounts for each brand, SendMails lets you manage multiple domains under one account at no extra cost.

What about list management and segmentation?

SendMails offers unlimited lists and segments without charging for duplicate contacts (unlike MailChimp). Our advanced segmentation includes behavioral triggers, custom fields, and dynamic lists. You can also use tags and custom attributes for precise targeting.

Is it difficult to learn your platform?

Our interface is modern and user-friendly, with similar concepts to MailChimp but better organization. We provide comprehensive documentation and video tutorials to help you get started.

What happens if Amazon SES has issues?

SendMails includes SMTP rotation capabilities, allowing you to configure backup providers. While Amazon SES is highly reliable, this gives you an extra layer of redundancy. Our system automatically handles failover if needed.

How secure is SendMails?

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including: encrypted data storage, secure authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with email security best practices. Your data is hosted on secure AWS infrastructure with regular backups.

Can I import my email templates?

Yes! You can import your HTML templates directly or recreate them using our drag-and-drop editor. We also provide modern, mobile-responsive templates optimized for better engagement.
